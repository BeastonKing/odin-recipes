Indomie

Description

Indomie is a staple for Indonesian. From the young to the old, everybody loves them. The low price mixed with the amazing taste makes it great to eat as a 'snack' or even a full meal

In this recipe, we will make an Indomie soup (that's broth Indomie) with the addition of a boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 Indomie (broth one) of your choice
  • 1 Egg
  • Mustard greens
  • 2 pinches of fried onions

Steps

  1. Chop the mustard greens until you get a handful of them (or as much as you wish)
  2. Boil some water in a medium heat
  3. Put the Indomie and the mustard greens inside the boiling water
  4. Boil them for +-3 minutes
  5. Put them in a bowl, set aside
  6. Boil the egg +-2 minutes
  7. While the egg is boiling, add seasonings (included in the Indomie package) to the Indomie
  8. Add +-200 ml of hot water to the bowl of Indomie
  9. Mix well
  10. When the egg is finished, put it in the bowl
  11. Add fried onions as toppings
  12. Indomie is ready to serve
