Sambal Geprek

Description

Sambal or chilli paste is a must have addition when eating meals for Indonesian. Even some has taken a step further to say that you are not actually eating a full meal unless there is sambal. Sambal has tons of variations, like many food in Indonesia, really.

The recipe that we will discuss here is how to make sambal geprek. The "geprek" part comes from the fact that this sambal is commonly used as an addition to ayam geprek (literally means smashed chicken). Let's jump straight in!

Ingredients

  • 50 gr of cayenne pepper
  • 5-7 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp of cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mash peppers and garlics until it becomes a paste
  2. Add seasonings
  3. Put sambal in a small bowl or plate
  4. Heat cooking oil in a pan until it's very hot
  5. Pour the heated oil to the sambal, mix very well
  6. Sambal geprek is ready to serve
