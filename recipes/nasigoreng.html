Nasi Goreng

Description

Fried rice is a typical Indonesian food that has tons of variations. Although there are already various variations, in a nutshell, fried rice is rice that is fried and then added with various spices and proteins for more enjoyment.

Commonly, fried rice is made using ingredients that can easily be found, or just leftovers. Today, we will make a fried rice that can be made with ease, but also tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 portion of white rice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Sambal (chilli paste)
  • Oyster sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • A green onion, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 chicken sausage
  • 3 tbsp of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat a pan with medium heat, and then add cooking oil
  2. Add in garlic and green onion that has already been chopped. Saute until golden colored
  3. Add sausage and egg, fry for 20 seconds
  4. Add rice, mix well
  5. Add salt, oyster sauce, sambal, and sweet soy sauce to taste. Mix well
  6. Serve on a plate
